===== HOW THE SALES PIPELINE WORKS =====

The full automated sales pipeline (Starter/Growth/Agency plans):

1. LEAD CAPTURE — Leads come in from website forms, Facebook/Instagram ads, Apollo B2B prospecting, social media DMs, or inbound phone calls
2. LEAD SCORING — AI scores every lead 0-100 against the client's ideal customer profile
3. APPOINTMENT SETTING — Hot leads get automated SMS/email follow-up within seconds, AI books them into the client's real calendar
4. CLOSING CALL (Agency plan) — AI voice agent calls at the booked appointment time, confirms the meeting, answers questions, pitches the offer, handles objections, and guides the prospect through payment while on the call
5. POST-CLOSE — Confirmation email and SMS sent, follow-up scheduled, referral ask

For AI Receptionist clients (service businesses):
1. CALL COMES IN — AI receptionist answers, qualifies, books appointment
2. FOLLOW-UP — 2 days after appointment, AI calls to check in and rebook if needed
3. RECURRING REMINDERS — AI calls when next appointment is due (based on business type)
4. PIPELINE TRACKING — NEW_INQUIRY → APPOINTMENT_BOOKED → COMPLETED → FOLLOW_UP_DUE → RECURRING_CLIENT

===== HOW THE CLOSER WORKS =====

The Voice Closer (Agency plan) is the appointment itself:
- The appointment setter books a time with the prospect
- At the booked time, the AI closer calls automatically
- Opens with: "Hey [name], we had this call booked in — glad we could connect!"
- Asks what made them interested, discovers their pain
- Pitches using the client's knowledge base (product info, pricing, benefits)
- Handles objections using proven frameworks
- When they say yes: "Let's get you set up right now while we're on the call. Are you near a browser?"
- Walks them through the sign-up/payment page step by step while staying on the line
- Only sends SMS/email link as fallback if they can't complete payment right now
- After payment confirmed: sets expectations, books check-in, asks for referral
